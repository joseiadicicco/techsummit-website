Healthy Gamification System

Beyond Credentials: Engaging Students Through Incentives

In addition to digital credentials, we will implement healthy gamification systems that benefit both universities and students.

Benefits for Universities

  • Save costs on printing physical diplomas
  • Increase student engagement and performance
  • Track and reward student achievements
  • Modernize the educational experience

Benefits for Students

  • Incentives for performance, achievements, and effort during coursework
  • Benefits for outstanding students based on performance level
  • Access to different benefits according to performance level achieved
  • Healthy competition and motivation

Proven Impact on Student Engagement

Gamification systems have demonstrated enormous impact on student engagement. Examples include:

  • Discord Orbes: Recently implemented gamification system that increased user engagement
  • Midnight Ambassadors: Implemented through Zealy.io, a gamification system to incentivize healthy competition among ambassadors and motivate participation

Transaction Impact: These solutions will increase transactions not only through student diplomas, but also through the uses students will make to access these benefits, creating additional transaction volume on the Cardano network.
