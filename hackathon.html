Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have a team?

No! You can participate individually or form teams during the event. Teams can have up to 5 members.

Do I need to complete the Developers Course first?

Not required! While course participants are welcome, the hackathon is open to all developers with blockchain experience.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, chargers, and enthusiasm! We'll provide internet, food, and workspace.

Is there a registration fee?

No! The hackathon is completely free to attend.
